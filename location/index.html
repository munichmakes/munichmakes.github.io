The Location

How to find MCSM

The MSCM is located at Sirius Business Park (form. Siemenswerke) at Rupert-Mayer-Str. 44. You can get there either with U3, station "Obersendling", or S3, station "Siemenswerke". It is a 5-10 minutes walk to the main entrance.

Finding Geb. 6415, Raum 169

Consult the following map to find Makerspace rooms:

Once you are witin the Sirius Business Park and look for building 6415. Enter the building, and go to the basement and follow the signs of "Modellbauclub der Siemens Mitarbeiter München"

Additional information how to get there are on the old MCSM website
